Exchange in The Netherlands?

- The home of bicycles and tulips -

The Netherlands, or more informally Holland, is a country located in Northwestern Europe. Netherlands literally means "lower countries", which is not a coincidence since 26% its land is below the sea level. Netherlands is know for its innovative education methods and is consistently ranking high among the best countries in Europe to study abroad. If you pursue an education in the Netherlands you get to experience visionary architecture, contemporary fashion, and tons of street markets!
